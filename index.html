Why
Heritage City?
Maharashtra is a state in India with a rich cultural and historical heritage, and it is home to several cities and sites that hold great historical and cultural significance.
Maharashtra is home to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, such as the Ajanta and Ellora Caves in Aurangabad, which are renowned for their ancient rock-cut temples and sculptures. These sites are considered part of the world's cultural heritage.
